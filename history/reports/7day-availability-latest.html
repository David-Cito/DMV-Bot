7-Day Appointment Availability Analysis

Metrics Explanation

This analysis tracks appointments that become available within 7 days.

Key Metrics:

  1. Availability Persistence: Percentage of appointments still available on the next check (5-30 min later). Higher = better chance to book.
    Interpretation: >80% = Very stable, 60-80% = Moderate, <60% = Highly competitive.
  2. Slot Count Trends: How many appointment slots appear at once when openings become available. More slots = better booking opportunity.
    Interpretation: Average >5 = Good, 3-5 = Moderate, <3 = Limited availability.
  3. Multi-Date Availability: When appointments appear within 7 days, how often multiple dates appear at the same time. Higher % = more options.
  4. Cancellation Timing: Peak hours when cancellations occur (creating openings). Useful for timing checks when new appointments are likely.
  5. Lead Time Trends: Whether appointments are getting closer (better) or further out (worse) over time. Shows overall availability trends.
  6. Competition Indicators: How quickly appointments disappear after appearing. Faster = more competition.
    Interpretation: <30 min = HIGH, 30-120 min = MODERATE, >120 min = LOW competition.
  7. Duration Statistics: How long appointments stay available once they appear within 7 days. Longer durations = more time to book.
    Interpretation: <1 hour = Very competitive, 1-6 hours = Competitive, >6 hours = Less competitive.
